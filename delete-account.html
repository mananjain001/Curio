Delete Your Curio Account

If you wish to delete your Curio account and all associated personal data, you can follow the steps below. This page ensures transparency in accordance with data privacy regulations and Google Play policies.

Steps to Request Account Deletion:

  1. Open the Curio app on your device.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings.
  3. Tap on “Delete My Account”.
  4. Confirm your request by selecting “Yes, Delete”.

If you face any issues or cannot access the app, you can also email us at support@curioapp.in with the subject line “Delete My Curio Account”.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account:

  • All your personal data (name, email, personality results, preferences, matches, and messages) will be permanently deleted.
  • Your account will no longer be accessible, and you will be logged out from all sessions.
  • Some non-personal, anonymized data may be retained for analytical and security purposes, in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
  • Backup data may be retained for up to 30 days for recovery and legal compliance, after which it is deleted.

For more information, please review our Privacy Policy.
