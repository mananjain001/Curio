Policy Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)
At Curio, we are fully committed to ensuring a safe and respectful experience for all users. We have a zero-tolerance policy toward any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), including the creation, sharing, or promotion of any content or behavior that harms or endangers children.

Our Commitments
We strictly prohibit any content that involves or promotes child sexual abuse or exploitation.

We do not permit users who attempt to engage in or facilitate such behavior on our platform.

We monitor and moderate user activity to detect and act on any such violations.

We report any discovered or suspected illegal content or behavior to appropriate law enforcement agencies and child protection organizations.

We cooperate with government authorities and comply with all relevant child protection laws and regulations.
